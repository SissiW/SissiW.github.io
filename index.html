Biography

In June 2024, I received the Ph.D. degree from [Anhui University] of China under the supervision of Professors Luo Bin and Jiang Bo. In July of the same year, I served as a postdoctor in [School of Computer Science and Technology] at Anhui University.

My current research interests are mainly graph based visual representation learning, few-shot learning and object re-identification. I have published over 15 papers in top journals and conferences, including IJCV, IEEE T-MM, T-CSVT，T-GRS，AAAI and CVPR. I am a reviewer for a number of journals, such as the IEEE TRANSACTIONS ON CIRCUITS AND SYSTEMS FOR VIDEO TECHNOLOGY, Parttern Recognition. For more information, see: https://SissiW.github.io/.

汪茜茜，安徽师范大学讲师。2024年6月，获得博士学位，师从罗斌教授和江波教授。同年7月，进入安徽大学计算机科学与技术学院做博士后。 主要研究方向是结构模式识别、小样本学习和目标重识别等。 在国内外高水平期刊和会议发表论文15余篇，包括IJCV、IEEE T-MM、IEEE T-CSVT、IEEE T-GRS、AAAI和CVPR等，其中以第一作者身份发表人工智能旗舰期刊IJCV论文2篇。 担任主流期刊的审稿人，包括IEEE TCSVT, PR等。
